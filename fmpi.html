Free Menstrual Product Initiative (FMPI)

This initiative is a joint collaboration between the Engineering Student Council and the Coalition for the Institutionalization of Free Menstrual Products to provide free menstrual products for UC Berkeley students, visitors, and alumni. This initiative is currently looking for donations and support to ensure that we (CIFMP and ESC) can continue this initiative for years to come. Please email us at esc@esc.berkeley.edu to let us know if you have concerns or comments about this initiative!
